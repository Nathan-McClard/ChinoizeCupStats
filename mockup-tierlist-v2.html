Leaders are ranked by a composite score that weights multiple performance signals to provide a holistic view of competitive viability. The system balances raw win rate against consistency and meta presence.

Score = (WR × 0.35) + (Top4% × 0.30) + (Entries_norm × 0.20) + (Wins_bonus × 0.15)

Win Rate (35%) measures overall match win percentage across all tracked tournaments in the current format. Only matches with verified results are included.

Top 4 Rate (30%) measures how often a leader finishes in the top 4 of a tournament relative to its total entries. This rewards consistent deep runs over raw volume.

Entries (20%) is normalized against the most-played leader in the format. Higher representation increases confidence in the win rate data and provides a meta-share signal.

Tournament Wins (15%) provides a bonus for first-place finishes. Each win is weighted equally regardless of tournament size. Leaders with < 5 entries in the current format are placed in U Tier (Unranked) due to insufficient sample size.

Tier boundaries are determined by natural breaks in the score distribution using a modified Jenks optimization algorithm, ensuring that leaders within a tier are more similar to each other than to leaders in adjacent tiers.
