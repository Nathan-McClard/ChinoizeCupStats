Tier Score is a composite metric that weighs win rate, top 4 conversion rate, and tournament wins, normalized against the field. Leaders need a minimum of 5 entries across tracked tournaments to qualify for ranking.

S Tier (Score 75+): Meta-defining leaders with dominant win rates and consistent top finishes. These are the decks to beat.

A Tier (Score 55-74): Strong competitive choices that regularly place well. Solid picks for any tournament.

B Tier (Score 40-54): Viable but not dominant. Can win in the right hands or meta pockets.

C Tier (Score 20-39): Below average results. Playable but face uphill battles against the meta.

Unranked: Fewer than 5 entries or insufficient data to generate a meaningful ranking.

Data sourced from ChinoizeCup tournaments during the EB-03 format. Updated after each event.
